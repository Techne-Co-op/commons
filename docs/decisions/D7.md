## Question

How we cite bylaw sections before ratification.

## Context and options

Pre-ratification, every citation uses the draft-bylaws:§x.y prefix against a register; ratification (R1) triggers one sweep that drops the prefix and freezes addresses. Keeps cite-as-you-enforce honest before the text is final.

## Proposed default

Stated in the context above; a proposal only. The decision is reserved to the named owner.

## Record of decision

Decided 2026-06-12 by Todd Youngblood (Ventures & Operations Steward).

Citations to Draft Bylaws and Member Agreements reference v2 of those documents as published at techne.institute/legal. Version is the addressing anchor — not a section-prefix scheme. When a new version is ratified, citations update to the new version number. This approach is simpler than the proposed prefix register and ties citations to the canonical published source, giving counsel and members a stable URL to verify against. The draft-bylaws:§x.y prefix scheme is not adopted.
