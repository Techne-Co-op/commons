## Question

Which entity holds the third-floor lease.

## Context and options

Whether West or the LCA holds the lease affects how facility obligations enter the ledger (CIS §17). Blocks specific W3.6 detail only; indexed so the dependency is visible.

## Proposed default

Stated in the context above; a proposal only. The decision is reserved to the named owner.

## Record of decision

Decided 2026-06-12 by Todd Youngblood (Ventures & Operations Steward).

The question is moot. The third floor has been occupied by another tenant; RegenHub, LCA does not hold and will not hold the third-floor lease. The cooperative operates from the second floor. No facility obligation for the third floor enters the CIS ledger. W3.6 milestone detail is updated accordingly — facility obligations reflect second-floor operations only.
