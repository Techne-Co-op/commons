## Question

Which events go on-chain, and who holds the keys.

## Context and options

Deferred beyond alpha. Blocks the optional attestation steps in W4 and W6 only.

## Proposed default

Stated in the context above; a proposal only. The decision is reserved to the named owner.

## Record of decision

Decided 2026-06-12 by Todd Youngblood (Ventures & Operations Steward).

Public ledger attestation is deferred beyond alpha — no events go on-chain during W0 through W2, and the optional attestation steps in W4 and W6 remain deferred until scope and key custody are fully deliberated by the working group. In the interim, tamper-evidence is achieved at the database layer via a local merkle tree: event rows are hashed and chained so that any retrospective modification is detectable without a public ledger dependency. This gives the cooperative a verifiable audit trail before any on-chain commitment is made, and without the key custody question needing resolution first.
